About & Guide

What It Is

Welcome to the Movie Recommender Web App! This tool helps you find new movies to watch based on your ratings and other users’ preferences using the cosine similarity algorithm.

How It Works

  • The app fetches movies from TMDb's Popular Movies list.
  • The movies list is updated constantly, your rating for the new movies is set to 0.
  • You rate the movies you’ve watched from 0 (not watched) to 5 (loved it).
  • Anything other that 1-5 is consiedered 0.
  • The system compares your ratings with other users and finds the most similar profiles.
  • Movies you haven’t rated but similar users rated highly are recommended to you.

Tips for Best Results

  • Rate at least 5–10 movies for more accurate recommendations.
  • Use the “Load More” button to explore additional movies from TMDb.
  • Click on a movie poster to search for it on Google for more details.

Data Privacy

Your ratings are stored only for recommendation purposes and are not shared publicly.
